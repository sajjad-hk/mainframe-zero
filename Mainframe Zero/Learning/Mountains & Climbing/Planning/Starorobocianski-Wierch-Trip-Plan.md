#### Morning: Dolina Kościeliska (~3–4h)

Easy recovery walk with caves and a canyon — completely different vibe from Day 1.

**Start:** Kiry parking (30–50 zł/day), ~10 min drive from Witów.

**Route:**

- [ ] **~08:00** — Start at Kiry, green trail into Dolina Kościeliska
- [ ] **~08:25** — Polana Stare Kościeliska — Kapliczka Zbójnicka, quick photo stop
- [ ] **~09:00** — Junction to Jaskinie Pawlikowskiego — turn right uphill (red/black trail)
- [ ] **~09:15** — **Jaskinia Raptawicka** — chains on approach, steel ladder descent into the cave. Unusual downward entry, spacious main chamber lit by natural light. Viewpoint outside on the way back. ~30 min detour total
- [ ] **~09:45** — Back to valley floor, continue up the valley
- [ ] **~10:15** — **Wąwóz Kraków + Smocza Jama** — yellow trail loop. Narrow canyon with ladders, chains, and a 37m rock tunnel cave. One-directional. ~30 min loop
- [ ] **~10:45** — Optional: continue to **Schronisko PTTK na Hali Ornak** (~20 min) for szarlotka + coffee
- [ ] **~11:15** — Optional: **Smreczyński Staw** — quiet forest lake, ~30 min from schronisko (black trail). Unique baggy-shore lake surrounded by spruce forest, very few tourists
- [ ] **~12:30** — Walk back to Kiry
- [ ] **~13:30** — Back at car

**Stats:** ~13 km flat (round trip), ~250m elevation gain. Easy pace.

**What to bring:**

- Czołówka (headlamp) + backup light — for Raptawicka (no electric lighting inside)
- Warm layer — caves are ~5°C inside even in summer
- Clothes you don't mind getting slightly dirty
- Cash for TPN entry (~9 zł) and parking

**Cave notes:**

- Jaskinia Raptawicka: 536m total, self-guided, no technical gear needed. Approach has chains + 4m steel ladder. Can turn back anytime
- Smocza Jama: 37m tunnel in Wąwóz Kraków, chains inside, one-directional loop
- Skipping Jaskinia Mroźna (lit tourist cave, crowded, less interesting) and Mylna (been there before)

#### Afternoon: Termy Szaflary (~14:00–18:00)

Most intimate and quiet thermal complex on Podhale. ~20 min drive from Kiry (toward Nowy Targ).

**Key info:**

| Detail     | Info                                                             |
| ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Location   | Szaflary (direction Nowy Targ)                                   |
| Website    | [termyszaflary.com](https://www.termyszaflary.com/)              |
| Hours      | 9:00–22:00 daily                                                 |
| Pools      | 4 pools (2 indoor, 2 outdoor) — highest mineral water on Podhale |
| Saunarium  | Finnish, infrared, herbal, aromatic, steam saunas                |
| Price      | Pool + sauna whole day ~189 zł                                   |
| Vibe       | Calm, far fewer people than Chochołowskie or Bania               |
| Bring      | Swimsuit, flip-flops, towel. Card payments accepted              |
| Restaurant | Pasta La Vista on-site (Italian + regional)                      |

**Why Szaflary over Chochołowskie:** Much smaller, no aquapark crowds, strongest mineral water in the region — ideal for sore post-summit muscles. The saunarium is compact and peaceful.

**Alternative nearby:** Gorący Potok (also Szaflary) — Japanese-style wooden complex, unchlorinated UV-filtered thermal water, outdoor pools. More atmospheric but can get lively in evenings.

## Notes

- The route is not technically difficult but very long and demanding on endurance
- The final approach to the summit involves steep switchbacks on loose stones — good boots are essential
- Emergency escape at Siwa Przełęcz — black trail descends directly to Dolina Chochołowska (~1h 20min)
- Alternative descent: retrace via Kończysty → Trzydniowiański (shorter but steep on knees)
- Fill water at streams in the valley before ascending — no water sources on the ridge
